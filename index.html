About


I am a guest professor and Postdoctoral Research Fellow of Research Foundation Flanders (FWO) at Ghent University. I obtained the title `Doctor of Economics' in January 2020 for my thesis titled "Firm Heterogeneity in International Trade" .

My research focuses on the topics of International Economics and Applied Microeconomics. My current work concentrates on the definition, characterization and measurement of firm-level heterogeneity in international trade.
